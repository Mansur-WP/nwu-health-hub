Services

Smart Campus Healthcare

From booking to billing, NWU HealthHub covers the full care workflow in one place.

Smart Appointments

Students and staff book in seconds. University doctors approve, cancel, or review — with automatic status updates.

Digital Prescriptions

Doctors write prescriptions saved as drafts. One click sends them to the campus pharmacy queue for dispensing.

Pharmacy Workflow

Pharmacists see a live queue of pending prescriptions and dispense from tracked inventory with stock alerts.

Payment Tracking

Auto-generated invoices on every booking. Admins verify payments in bulk. Patients view full billing history.

Role-Based Access

Every user sees only what they need. Four distinct portals: Admin, Doctor, Patient, and Pharmacist.

Admin Dashboard

Live KPI tiles, appointment status charts, and recent activity — everything an administrator needs at a glance.
